# Vertical Memory — School

## Query patterns

- "school {geo}"
- "best school in {geo}"
- "cbse school {geo}"
- "icse school {geo}"
- "top school near {geo}"
- "international school {geo}" (for IB/IGCSE clients)

## Standard sections for all school landing pages

- Header (Logo + Phone + Admission Enquiry CTA) — mandatory
- Hero (Headline + Admission CTA, often with school imagery) — mandatory
- About the School (founding year, philosophy, affiliation)
- Programs / Classes Offered (Nursery → Grade 12, with board name)
- Faculty (credentials, experience, student-teacher ratio)
- Infrastructure / Campus (labs, library, sports, auditorium)
- Achievements & Awards (board results, sports, olympiad)
- Testimonials (Parents + Alumni stories)
- Gallery (campus, events, activities)
- Admission Process (steps, key dates, fee structure hint)
- FAQ — mandatory, minimum 6 items — must appear BEFORE Contact Form
- Contact Form / Admission Enquiry Form — mandatory
- Footer (address, phone, email, map) — mandatory

## Trust signal categories

- CBSE affiliation number (if CBSE school)
- ICSE / ISC affiliation (if ICSE school)
- IB World School authorization
- IGCSE Cambridge affiliation
- Year established
- Student enrollment / school strength
- Board exam pass percentage (Class 10 / Class 12)
- Rank or recognition from state/national education body
- Sports achievements (national/state level)
- Olympiad / academic competition results
- Alumni achievements (IIT/NEET/Civil Services selections)

## National chains / large brands to exclude from competitor search

Pass as `--exclude-domains` to firecrawl_search.py:

- delhipublicschool.in (DPS chain)
- ryaninternational.com
- davpublicschool.in
- kendriyavidyalaya.nic.in
- navodaya.gov.in
- sanskriti school (large chain)

Also exclude directories: schoolmykids.com, indiaschools.com, justdial.com

## Competitor red flags (missed = opportunity for our client)

- No admission CTA above fold
- Board affiliation not mentioned prominently
- No board results / pass percentage
- No faculty credentials or photos
- Outdated gallery (photos more than 2 years old)
- No student-teacher ratio
- No infrastructure detail
- Admission process not explained
- FAQ section missing
- No map / address in footer

## CTA norms

Most common CTAs in schools (descending frequency):
- "Admission Enquiry"
- "Apply Now"
- "Book a School Tour"
- "Contact Us"
- "Download Prospectus"
